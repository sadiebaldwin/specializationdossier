Technical Skill Snapshot

Key technical competencies for emerging technologies work

No longer are librarians just keepers of card catalogs and books. The digital age has ushered in a new set of requirements in order to stay current in the field. Along with interpersonal skills to relate to patrons, librarians and information professionals must develop and acquire certain technical skills in this day and age to meet their institution’s needs.

Emerging Technologies librarians must be at the forefront of this movement, taking advantage of journals, conferences, and associations to keep their skill sets up to date. Edwin Rodarte from LAPL stressed the importance of the following technical skills:

Key Technical Skills

  • Project management
  • Grant writing
  • Data analysis (Excel courses)
  • Power BI
  • Tableau
  • Google Looker (especially dashboarding)
  • Code learning (like HTML)
